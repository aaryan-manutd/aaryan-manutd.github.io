Trips with F.R.I.E.N.D.S.

Ooty

I with my friends visited Ooty, which is located in the Nilgiri Mountain Ranges. This place is one of the most famous tourist destination for people all over India. Totally pristine & peaceful.

Tada

Tada is a waterfall situated in the neighbour state of Andra Pradesh and is about 120 kms away from Chennai. Many college students from nearby states likes to visit here and enjoy the beauty of nature.

Mahabalipuram

Located on the Coromandel Coast of the Bay of Bengal, about 60 kms south of Chennai, Mahabalipuram is a historic city & UNESCO World Heritage site. It has numerous ancient temples, architectures and monuments depicting the ancient culture of South India and also a really pristine beach which offer a peaceful & serene ambience.
