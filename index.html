Mahabalipuram

Located on the Coromandel Coast of the Bay of Bengal, about 60 kms south of Chennai, Mahabalipuram is a historic city & UNESCO World Heritage site. It has numerous ancient temples, architectures and monuments depicting the ancient culture of South India and also a really pristine beach which offer a peaceful & serene ambience.
